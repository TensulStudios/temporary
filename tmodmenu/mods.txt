[Player Mods]

Player Info - Shows info such as platform, id etc.
Mod Check - Detects mods.
Goto - Teleports you to the target.
Headsit - Constantly teleports you to the target.
Lag - Lags the target.
Tag - Tags the target (if you are tagged).

[Movement]

Fly - Hold [A] to fly forward.
Teleport Gun - Hold [GRIP] and [TRIGGER] to teleport.
Wall Walk - Pulls you towards the wall.
Speed Boost - Gives you a speed boost.
Invisible [B] - Hides your rig down.
Ghost [A] - Freezes your rig.
Noclip [GRIP] - Disables all colliders.

[Safety]

Anti Report - Disconnects you when a player is near your report button.

[Visual]

Chams - Lets you see player fur through walls.

[Advantage]
Lag All - Lags all.
Lag Gun - Lags a target.
Tag All - Tags all.
Tag Gun - Tags a target.
