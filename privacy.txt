Temporary Studios does not collect any personal information directly from players. Our games use third-party services, PlayFab and Photon, which may collect limited data needed for gameplay features such as player authentication and multiplayer networking. We do not access, store, or share this data ourselves.

For more information, please review their privacy policies:
PlayFab: https://privacy.microsoft.com/en-us/privacystatement
Photon: https://dashboard.photonengine.com/account/privacyandcookiepolicy

If you would like us to remove your data from any of our games, please contact us at request@temporarystudios.org
